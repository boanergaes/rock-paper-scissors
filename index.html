About the Game

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple and fun hand game played between two players â€” in this case, you vs. the computer.

    Each player chooses one of three moves:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

    • If both players choose the same move, the round ends in tie.

How the Game Works

In this version of the game:

  • The game consists of 7 rounds
  • In each round:
    • If you win, you earn 10 pts.
    • If the computer wins, it earns 10 pts.
    • If it's a tie or you lose, you gain no pts.
  • At the end of the 7 rounds:
    • If your total score is higher, you win the game.
    • If the computer scores higher, you lose.
    • If both scores are equal, it's a tie.

At the end of each full game (after 7 rounds), your result along with your and the computer's scores will be saved. You can find your past games in the History Page.

How to Play

  • You will see three buttons on the bottom of the screen -- one for each move: Rock, Paper, and Scissors.
  • Click any one of them to make your move for the current round.
  • The computer will automatically pick its move, and the result of the round will be displayed immediately.
  • After 7 rounds, your final score and game result will be shown.

Navigating the Website

In the top-right corner of the page, you'll find four helpful buttons:

  • History -- View the results of all completed games.
  • Help -- You're here now!
  • About -- Learn about this project and it's developer.
  • Theme -- Switch between light mode and dark mode.

Let the games begin -- and may the best mobe win!
